Saurav Ambuskar

Saurav Ambuskar is a visionary entrepreneur, project strategist, and creative professional with a

His vision for Vyakti Parichay emerged from a simple yet powerful belief: every individual's

Through Vyakti Parichay, he aims to create a trusted platform where success stories are

Co-Founder

Nagesh Patil is a digital marketing professional, entrepreneur, branding consultant, and

With years of experience in social media marketing, online advertising, branding, content

As the Co-Founder of Vyakti Parichay, Nagesh strongly believes that every person has a unique

story worth sharing and every achievement deserves visibility.

His expertise in digital marketing and personal branding plays a crucial role in helping

individuals showcase their accomplishments effectively and professionally.

His mission is to ensure that individuals receive the recognition they deserve while building

meaningful digital identities that create long-term value.
